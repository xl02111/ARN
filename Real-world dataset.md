# Real-world dataset

We construct a real-world dataset for testing. Nine volunteers are recruited  to shoot in 6 kinds of scenes of the campus contained indoor and outdoor at 6-9 pm.We collect 226 images in total under low-light conditions with 200 images for gallery and 26 images for query. Due to factors such as low light and exposure of street lights, low-light images in real-world scenes will be more complicated. In order to prove the effectiveness of this method in reducing the effects of low light, we conducted experiments on our real-world dataset.Some examples are shown as follow：
